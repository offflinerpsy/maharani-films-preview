THE STUDIO

Maharani Films is a fine-art studio for the ceremonies you grew up with.

We bring the quiet confidence of European editorial photography to the colour and emotion of South Asian weddings — understood ritual by ritual, filmed day by day, by a team that has stood in these rooms many times before.

Cultural Fluency

We know the flow and meaning of every ceremony, and we understand the languages spoken in the room. Nothing is missed, because nothing is unfamiliar.

A Full Production Team

A South Asian wedding is many events across many days. We arrive as a coordinated crew led by one dedicated producer — so the bride's suite, the Baraat, and the stage are all covered at once.

An Editorial Legacy

As part of the Dreamwood family of studios, we work with restraint and true colour — imagery and film made to feel timeless, and to age beautifully.
